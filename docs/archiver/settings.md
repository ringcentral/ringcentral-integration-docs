# Managing Archiver Settings and Permissions  

When RingCentral Archiver is enabled, admins and users can independently manage data archiving. Super Admins and Managers have default access to Archiver and can enable it for specific users.  

## Enabling Archiver for Admins and Users  

To connect a cloud storage account or SFTP server, Archiver must be enabled for admins and users. Once enabled, users can access Archiver to connect and sync accounts for data archiving.  

### To enable Archiver:  

1. Sign in to the **Admin Portal**.  
2. Click **More**.  
3. Select **Account Settings (a)**, then click **Archiver Settings (b)**.  
4. Toggle **Admin Archiver** and/or **User Archiver** to ON.  

## Granting Archiver Access to a Custom Role  

Super Admins can create custom roles with permissions to manage Archiver settings.  

### To create a custom role with Archiver access:  

1. Sign in to the **Admin Portal**.  
2. Click the **Users** tab.  
3. Select **Roles**.  
4. Click **New Role**, choose an existing role as a template, then click **Next**.  
5. Enter a **name** and **description** for the role, then click **Next**.  
6. Scroll to the **Features** section and check **Archiver for Admins**.  
7. Click **Create Role**.
