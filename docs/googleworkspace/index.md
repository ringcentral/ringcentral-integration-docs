# RingCentral for Google Workspace

## Overview

RingCentral for Google Workspace seamlessly integrates communication capabilities into your Gmail and Google Calendar environment. This powerful add-on streamlines your workflow by bringing phone, video, and messaging features directly into the Google applications you use every day, eliminating the need to switch between different communication platforms.

## Key Features

- **Gmail Integration**: Make calls directly from email contacts and phone numbers
- **Click-to-call**: Initiate phone calls from any phone number in Gmail or Google Calendar
- **Video Meeting Scheduling**: Schedule RingCentral video meetings directly from Google Calendar
- **Meeting Links**: Automatically add RingCentral meeting details to calendar invitations
- **Contact Synchronization**: Access your RingCentral contacts within Google applications
- **Call History**: View recent calls and communication history
- **Presence Integration**: See colleague availability status in real-time

## Benefits

- **Unified Communication**: Manage phone, video, and email from one interface
- **Enhanced Productivity**: Reduce app switching and streamline workflows  
- **Seamless Scheduling**: Create video meetings with automatic calendar integration
- **Cross-platform Access**: Works across desktop and mobile Google applications
- **Easy Collaboration**: Share meeting links and dial-in information automatically

## Getting Started

The Google Workspace Add-on integrates smoothly with your existing Gmail and Google Calendar workflow. Installation is straightforward through the Google Workspace Marketplace, and setup takes just a few minutes to connect your RingCentral account.
