# Building your first RingCentral Zap

## Logging in and Creating Your First Zap

Follow these steps to set up your first Zap using RingCentral:

1. Log in to your Zapier account.
2. Click “+ Create Zap” in the top-left corner of the Zapier home page.
3. Choose RingCentral as the trigger app.
4. Select a trigger event, such as “New Call Recording”.
5. Connect your RingCentral account by entering your credentials.
6. Test your trigger by making a test call and checking if Zapier detects it.

## Selecting an Action App

Once your trigger is set up, choose an action app that determines what happens next. For example, if you want to save call recordings to Google Drive:

* Select Google Drive as your action app.
* Choose Upload File as the action event.
* Connect your Google Drive account.
* Map data fields to ensure the correct information is stored.
* Test the action to confirm the file uploads successfully.

## Publishing Your Zap

Once your trigger and action are configured:

Click Publish Zap.

## Name your Zap and save it to a folder.

Ensure the Zap is turned ON.
