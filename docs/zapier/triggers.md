# RingCentral triggers in Zapier

Each trigger follows a similar setup process:

1. Select a RingCentral trigger, such as Missed Call or New Voicemail.
2. Connect your RingCentral account.
3. Perform a test action, such as missing a call or sending an SMS.
4. Confirm that Zapier detects the trigger.

Testing Different Triggers

1. Missed Call: Leave a missed call to your RingCentral account.
2. Call Ended: Make and end a call from your RingCentral account.
3. New Voicemail: Leave a voicemail.
4. New SMS Received: Send a text message to your RingCentral account.
5. New SMS Sent: Send a text message from your RingCentral account.
