# RingCentral for Microsoft Outlook

## Overview

RingCentral for Microsoft Outlook seamlessly integrates phone and video capabilities directly into your Outlook experience. This powerful add-in transforms your Outlook interface into a comprehensive communication hub, enabling you to make calls, schedule video meetings, and manage your communications without leaving your email environment.

## Key Features

- **Click-to-call**: Make phone calls directly from email addresses and contacts in Outlook
- **Video meeting scheduling**: Schedule RingCentral video meetings with automatic meeting details
- **Contact integration**: Access your RingCentral contacts within Outlook
- **Call history**: View recent calls and messages directly in Outlook
- **Presence indicators**: See colleague availability status in real-time
- **Meeting management**: Join scheduled meetings with one click

## Benefits

- **Streamlined workflow**: Eliminate app switching between Outlook and RingCentral
- **Enhanced productivity**: Manage all communications from one familiar interface
- **Seamless scheduling**: Create video meetings with automatic calendar integration
- **Unified experience**: Access phone, video, and email capabilities in one place

## Getting Started

The RingCentral for Microsoft Outlook add-in is designed to enhance your existing Outlook workflow by adding powerful communication capabilities directly where you work. Installation and setup are straightforward, allowing you to start communicating more efficiently right away.
